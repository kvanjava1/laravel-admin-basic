## Design

- Full viewport height center layout (navbar + footer tetap visible)
- "404" in `text-9xl font-extrabold text-slate-200` (watermark style)
- "Page Not Found" in `text-2xl font-bold text-text-primary`
- Subtitle in `text-base text-text-secondary`
- Dua tombol:
  - "Back Home" → `/` (outline/ghost button)
  - "Browse Blog" → `/articles` (primary solid button)
- Background: `bg-background-light`

## SEO

- Title: "404 — Page Not Found"
- Meta robots: `noindex, nofollow`
